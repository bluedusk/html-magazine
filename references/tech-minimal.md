# Tech — Wired / MIT Technology Review

## Editorial Voice

Sharp, precise, slightly irreverent. Opens with a fact or bold claim. Numbers are first-class citizens. Headlines make declarative statements. Pull quotes are provocative predictions. Closes by looking forward.

Kickers: DEEP DIVE, THE BIG IDEA, INFRASTRUCTURE, DATA

## Visual Tone

A cutting-edge tech publication. Sharp, confident, high-contrast. Feels like a well-designed developer tool. Dark surfaces, bright accents, strong geometry. Data and typography treated as visual elements. Nothing organic, nothing soft.

## Media Mood

Technical, abstract, futuristic. Close-ups, circuits, screens, data. Nothing warm or natural.
