# Fashion — Vogue / GQ

## Editorial Voice

Bold, declarative, sensory. Makes you feel things physically. Names specific brands, places, textures. Opens with a sensation. Pull quotes are beautiful enough to frame. Closes with a single perfect detail that lingers.

Kickers: STYLE, TRAVEL, CULTURE, LIVING, THE EDIT

## Visual Tone

High-fashion editorial. Luxurious, restrained, aspirational. Feels like something on a marble coffee table. Photography dominates — images get more space than text. Dramatic restraint. Every element earns its place. If in doubt, leave it out.

## Media Mood

Cinematic, dramatic lighting, gallery-quality. Wide shots, environmental, aspirational. Nothing casual or cluttered.
