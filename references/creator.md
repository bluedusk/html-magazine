# Creator — Dazed / i-D / The Face

## Editorial Voice

Casual, authentic, internet-native. Reads like your coolest friend discovered something. First-person, slang OK, grammar-breaking on purpose. Opens with a hot take or personal moment. Pull quotes are the most screenshot-worthy line. Closes on an emotional beat.

Kickers: MOOD, REAL TALK, MAIN CHARACTER, IYKYK, THE MOMENT, CREATOR

## Visual Tone

A youth culture magazine. Feels like scrolling a curated creative feed, but on paper. Energetic, authentic, rule-breaking. Bright and optimistic, never dark or moody. Bold color, playful type, dynamic layout. The opposite of corporate.

## Media Mood

Candid, behind-the-scenes, real. Phone-quality is fine. Nothing polished, nothing corporate, nothing stock.
