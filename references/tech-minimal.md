# Tech — Wired / MIT Technology Review

## Editorial Voice

Sharp, precise, slightly irreverent. Opens with a fact or bold claim. Numbers are first-class citizens — stats deserve visual prominence. Headlines make declarative statements. Pull quotes are provocative predictions. Closes by looking forward.

Kickers: DEEP DIVE, THE BIG IDEA, INFRASTRUCTURE, DATA

## Visual Tone

A cutting-edge tech publication. Sharp, confident, high-contrast. Everything feels like a well-designed developer tool. The surface behind the magazine is near-black. Pages are clean and bright against the dark surface. No soft edges, no rounded corners — geometry and whitespace.

### Typography Character
Body text is sans-serif — clean, modern, readable. Headlines are sans-serif, extremely bold (black weight), with very tight letter-spacing — commanding and direct. Subheads are sans-serif, bold, larger than usual, not uppercase. Kickers are sans-serif, uppercase, bold, very widely letter-spaced, in the accent color. Byline elements separated by " // ". Data and stats use monospace font. No drop cap — the first line of the opening paragraph is oversized and bold instead.

### Color Mood
Clean white pages against a near-black surface. Dark, near-black cover. Two accent colors — one hot/electric color for primary accents, one neon/bright color for data highlights. A gradient bar or rule using both accent colors is a signature element. Data callout boxes have dark backgrounds with large bright numbers.

### Layout Character
Single column preferred for readability. Data callout boxes with dark backgrounds and large bright monospace numbers — used for stats. Pull quotes are left-bordered with the accent color, indented. Section dividers are gradient horizontal rules. Images get thin accent-colored borders on one side. A gradient bar across the top of the cover is the signature element. Tags/topics list in monospace on the closing page.

## Media Mood
Technical, abstract, futuristic. Close-ups, circuits, screens, data visualization. Cover uses semi-transparent image behind dark overlay. Body images get thin accent borders. Nothing warm or organic.
